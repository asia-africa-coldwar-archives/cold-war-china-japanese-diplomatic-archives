controlled_vocabulary_events_v03 schema update

Added fields:
- parent_event_id
- edition_number
- event_status

Purpose:
- parent_event_id groups repeated meetings or event series.
- edition_number records the official/session number when available.
- event_status distinguishes held events, planned/postponed events, event series, and historical processes.

Current event_status values:
- held
- planned_postponed
- series
- historical_process

Assigned parent_event_id values:
- ASIAN_AFRICAN_CONFERENCE_SERIES
  - BANDUNG_1955: edition 1, held
  - SECOND_AA_CONF_1965_PLANNED: edition 2, planned_postponed
- NON_ALIGNED_SUMMIT_SERIES
  - NAM_SUMMIT_3_1970: edition 3, held
- UNGA_SESSION_SERIES
  - UNGA_30_1975: edition 30, held
- UNCTAD_SESSION_SERIES
  - UNCTAD_III_1972: edition 3, held

Special cases:
- AAPSO_CONFERENCE_SERIES has event_status=series and no edition_number.
- CULTURAL_REVOLUTION_1966_1976 has event_status=historical_process and no edition_number.

Note:
- parent_event_id values are series identifiers, not separate event rows yet. If needed later, they can become authority records in a separate event_series vocabulary.

Changes from supplied version:
- Added AFRO_ASIA for Bandung, Asian-African Conference, AAPSO, and Afro-Asian solidarity records.
- Added CARIBBEAN for Caribbean countries/territories in Latin America and voting-attitude files.
- Revised GLOBAL so UN/ICJ are not treated as regions; those should go in organization_ids.
- Regenerated this summary in UTF-8 to avoid mojibake in Japanese terms.

Design principles:
- region_ids describe the main geographic subject or geographic scale.
- reaction_region_ids can reuse this vocabulary for response/source regions, but should be stored in a separate column.
- UN, ICJ, UNGA, WMO, OAU etc. are organizations and should be stored in organization_ids.
- SOCIALIST_BLOC, WESTERN_BLOC, ARAB_WORLD, UNSC_MEMBERS etc. are blocs/groupings and should be stored in bloc_ids, not region_ids.
- Preserve historical source expressions such as 中近東, 南部アフリカ, and 中国・アジア諸国 in original_terms.

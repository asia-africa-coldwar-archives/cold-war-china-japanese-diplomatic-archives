review_queue_human_checklist_v01 summary

This checklist tells the human reviewer where to look and what to decide for the three open review queues:
- bloc_review_queue_v02.csv
- event_review_queue_v01.csv
- event_review_queue_cultural_revolution_v01.csv

Recommended order:
1. Confirm likely event assignments with direct current records: Cambodia 1981, UN China representation 1971, Sino-Indian mediation, AAPSO individual conferences.
2. Confirm bloc questions only where source wording indicates collective action.
3. Process Cultural Revolution candidates by checking dates first, then TOC/body context.
4. Search unmatched event candidates separately: UNGA 36 and Tricontinental Conference.

General rule:
- Do not assign bloc_ids or event_ids from association alone.
- Confirm with MOFA material-detail metadata, TOC, or document images.

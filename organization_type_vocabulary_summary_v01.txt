controlled_vocabulary_organization_types.csv summary

Created organization type authority vocabulary from the user-proposed categories:
- political_party
- government_in_exile
- national_liberation_movement
- united_front
- armed_movement
- international_organization
- negotiating_coalition

Design choice:
- organization_type_id is the normalized controlled field.
- organization_type remains the legacy/free-text detail field, e.g. UN organ, UN specialized agency, military alliance, transnational political organization.

Important distinction:
- negotiating_coalition can overlap with bloc_ids.
- In this database, Group of 77, Group B, African Group, Asian Group, and similar groups are usually better kept as bloc_ids unless a separate organization authority record is intentionally created.

Existing organization vocabulary update:
- Created controlled_vocabulary_organizations_v04_type_checked.csv.
- Existing formal organizations are currently normalized as organization_type_id=international_organization.
- AAPSO remains organization_type=transnational political organization, with normalized organization_type_id=international_organization because it is an organization authority record rather than a bloc record.

Future use:
- Add national_liberation_movement / armed_movement entries when named movements such as MPLA, FRELIMO, PAIGC, etc. are added.
- Add government_in_exile or united_front only when source wording supports those forms.

controlled_vocabulary_events_v06 update

Added field:
- related_bloc_ids_note

Rule:
- related_bloc_ids is a conceptual association only.
- Do not automatically copy related_bloc_ids into record-level bloc_ids.
- Record-level bloc_ids should be assigned only when the source explicitly treats the bloc/group as:
  - a collective actor
  - a common position
  - a voting group
  - a negotiating group
  - a historical source category, such as 「アジア自由圏」 or 「共産圏諸国」

Why this matters:
- BANDUNG_1955 may be conceptually related to AFRO_ASIAN_BLOC, but Bandung records should not automatically receive AFRO_ASIAN_BLOC.
- AAPSO_CONFERENCE_SERIES may be conceptually related to AFRO_ASIAN_BLOC, but AAPSO records should remain organization/event records unless bloc action is explicit.
- UNCTAD_III_1972 is conceptually related to G77, Group B, Asian Group, and African Group, but record-level bloc_ids require source-explicit negotiating group evidence.
- CULTURAL_REVOLUTION_1966_1976 is conceptually related to socialist/communist bloc categories, but those bloc_ids must not be assigned merely because the Cultural Revolution is tagged.
